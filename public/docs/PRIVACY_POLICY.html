Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection and Handling

Cisco IOS Insight is designed with privacy-first principles for network professionals. We do not store your configuration files on our servers. All configuration analysis is performed through a direct connection between your browser and the Google Gemini API.

2. Use of AI Services

The configurations you upload or paste are sent to the Google Gemini API (gemini-3-pro-preview) for processing. Please be aware that:

  • Sensitive data like plain-text passwords or SNMP community strings should be scrubbed before upload if you have specific corporate data-residency requirements.
  • Processing is subject to Google's Generative AI Data Processing Terms.

3. Local Storage

We may use local browser storage (LocalStorage) to store your preferences or session state. No network configuration data is persisted in local storage once the session is closed or reset.

4. Third-Party Links

Our audit reports may include links to external websites (e.g., Cisco Security Advisories or CVE databases). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

5. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please contact canti@firestartforge.dev.
