Privacy Policy for Cisco IOS Insight

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At Cisco IOS Insight, we prioritize the security and privacy of your network configurations. This policy outlines how data is handled within this application.

1. Local-First Philosophy

Cisco IOS Insight is designed as a client-side application. This means:

  • No Configuration Storage: Your Cisco configurations are processed in your browser's memory and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Local API Keys: Your Gemini API keys are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. They are never sent to any server other than Google's official Generative AI endpoints.

2. Third-Party Services (Google Gemini API)

When you perform an audit, the text of your Cisco configuration files is sent directly to Google's Gemini API servers.

  • This data transfer is necessary for the AI to analyze your configurations.
  • Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and the Gemini API Terms of Service for details.

3. No Tracking or Analytics

This application does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or any third-party marketing tools.

4. Security

While we ensure your data is handled securely within the browser, you are responsible for:

  • Keeping your API key confidential.
  • Ensuring the device you use to run this app is secure.
